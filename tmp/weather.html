Weather Page

This page contains links to weather pages. For those of you who are not ham radio operators, many of us love the weather and other interesting scientific topics. My wife and I love to spot storms. We also do what is commonly called "Storm Chase". We've spent a lot of our time in the summers chasing and spotting for our local Sky Warn Group and the National Weather Service in Pueblo, Co. Note that we DO go through the training given each year, and this is a rather dangerous hobby sometimes. So, don't just get interested in a hobby like this without being prepared for the danger! Know what to do and how to protect yourself first! Now you know the reason for weather pages! 73 -- Rick, NØNJY

There are several hundred links here. If any of them are found to be dead or no longer valid, please email me with the link name so I can make corrections or deletions. Thanks!
